How can we help?






Top FAQ

  • Membership

    • When can I access a location? What are W3Space's opening hours?
    The Community team is generally on-site at each location on local weekdays during business hours - between 9 AM and 6 PM. If you have a Private Office or a Dedicated Desk at a W3Space location, you have 24/7 access to that location. Other members need to book a meeting room or coworking space to access W3Spacelocations.

    Am I able to move to another W3Space location?
    Yes. You can work in different W3Space locations by booking coworking spaces or meeting rooms, subject to availability.

  • Operation

    • What is W3Space’s cancellation policy?
    For shared working space, you should cancel 3 days in advance online or by call; for meeting room and VIP room, cancellation should be done 7 days in advance.

    Is printing service offered?
    Yes, we offer printing service with charge.
